# Welcome

## Top Barks Dog Training

I am an experienced and highly qualified York based dog trainer who specialises in helping people to train their dogs and tackle a range of behaviour problems. Top Barks was established in 2006 with the aim of bringing people and their dogs together.

## So why choose Top Barks?

- Fun reward-based training
- One-to-one attention at a time to suit you
- I am a member of the Association of Pet Dog Trainers
- A York dog training business with a UK wide client base
- Recommended by veterinary practices throughout North Yorkshire
